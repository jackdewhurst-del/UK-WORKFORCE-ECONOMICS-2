What this is

A lightweight tool hub to model people-risk, retention pressure, attraction friction and the cost of “doing nothing”, using industry baselines plus your inputs. Designed to be screenshot/print friendly for internal business cases.

How to use it (fast)

1) Pick an industry (this loads predictive baseline assumptions).
2) Enter company headcount + salary assumptions on each page.
3) Test marginal improvements (e.g. -2pp turnover) and generate a printable report.

Open Retention model → Open Attraction model → Open Economics model →
Tip: use “Copy share link” to send a scenario to your boss/customer.

Tool hub roadmap (simple)

Keep it clean. One page per widget. Each widget should: (a) cite assumptions, (b) show baseline vs improved, (c) be printable.
